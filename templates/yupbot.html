YupBot

This site was created to provide SAAS (Software as A Service) to clients. You can use the plugin in any site to provide support to the customers using the chatbots. This service can be in the form of TEXT as well as SPEECH. The other use case of these chatbots can be done to get reviews or public insights. The surveyor can keep track of which questions were responded the most or who answered which questions or can even go through the chat history on his dashboard.
